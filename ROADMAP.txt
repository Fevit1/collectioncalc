**Why This Matters:**
- Legal obligation to report CSAM to NCMEC (18 U.S.C. ¬ß 2258A)
- Protect platform from abuse
- Zero tolerance for illegal/explicit content

**Phase 1: MVP (Before Private Beta)**

- [ ] Register for NCMEC CyberTipline ESP account (~1 week approval)

## Medium Priority

### Two-Factor Authentication (Backlog)
**Recommendation:** TOTP (authenticator app) - best security/simplicity balance

**Options ranked by complexity:**
1. **Email-based** (1-2 hrs) - Send 6-digit code, already have Resend
2. **TOTP** (2-3 hrs) - Google Authenticator, use `pyotp` library ‚Üê RECOMMENDED
3. **SMS** (ongoing cost) - Twilio, less secure than TOTP
4. **Passkeys/WebAuthn** (complex) - Most secure, most complex

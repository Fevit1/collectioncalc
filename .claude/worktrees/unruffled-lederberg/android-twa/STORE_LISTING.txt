APP TITLE (30 chars max):
Slab Worthy

SHORT DESCRIPTION (80 chars max):
AI-powered comic grading, valuation, and theft protection for collectors.

FULL DESCRIPTION:

Should you slab it? Now you'll know before you spend the money.

Slab Worthy uses AI to grade your comics in seconds. Just snap 4 photos — front cover, spine, back cover, and centerfold — and get an instant condition grade, fair market value, and a clear verdict on whether professional grading is worth the investment.

KEY FEATURES:

AI Comic Grading
Upload 4 photos and get a professional-quality grade in under 30 seconds. Our patent-pending multi-angle analysis detects spine stress, corner wear, color breaks, and dozens of other defects that affect your comic's value.

Fair Market Value
Get real-time valuations based on actual eBay and marketplace sales data. See what your comic is worth raw vs. slabbed, so you can make informed decisions.

Slab Guard Theft Protection
Register your comics with our patent-pending fingerprinting system. If your comics are ever stolen, Slab Guard monitors online marketplaces and alerts you when a match is found.

My Collection
Build and track your entire comic collection. See your total portfolio value, filter by era, sort by grade or value, and export your collection.

WHO IS THIS FOR?

Whether you're a casual collector wondering about that old box in the attic, or a serious dealer evaluating hundreds of books, Slab Worthy gives you the data you need to make smart decisions.

Two patents pending. Built by collectors, for collectors.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 16, 2026

Introduction

Slab Worthy ("we," "us," or "our") is a service provided by CollectionCalc. We're committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information when you use slabworthy.com and our related services.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password (stored securely using industry-standard hashing). You must be at least 16 years old to create an account.

Payment Information: When you subscribe to a paid plan, payment is processed by Stripe. We do not collect, store, or have access to your full credit card number. Stripe provides us with limited billing information (last 4 digits of your card, expiration date, billing address) for transaction records.

Comic Images: When you use our assessment feature, you upload photos of your comics. These images are processed by our AI to identify the comic and assess condition. Images are also screened for prohibited content using automated image analysis (see "Image Processing and Third-Party Services" below).

Slab Guard Registration Data: When you register a comic with Slab Guard, we generate and store a perceptual hash (digital fingerprint) of your comic's image, along with comic details (title, issue, grade) and a unique serial number. This fingerprint is a mathematical representation — it cannot be used to reconstruct your original photo.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our service, including comics assessed, valuations viewed, feature usage, and subscription activity. This helps us improve the service.

Technical Data: We automatically collect certain technical information including your IP address, browser type, and device information for security and analytics purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our comic assessment and valuation services
  • Process your uploaded images through our AI analysis system
  • Send you service-related emails (password resets, important updates)
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features and user experience
  • Protect against fraud and maintain security

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Image Processing and Third-Party Services

When you upload comic images, they are processed using multiple services. We want to be transparent about exactly what happens to your images:

Content Safety Screening (Amazon Web Services Rekognition): All uploaded images are automatically sent to AWS Rekognition for content moderation. This service analyzes images to detect prohibited content such as explicit material, violence, and other content that violates our policies. AWS Rekognition processes the image and returns labels/categories — it does not store the image after processing. We do not use any facial recognition features. This screening is mandatory and cannot be opted out of; it is required for us to maintain a safe platform and meet our legal obligations.

AI Grading Analysis (Anthropic Claude): Your images are sent to Anthropic's Claude AI for comic identification, condition assessment, and grading estimates. Anthropic processes the image in accordance with their privacy policy and does not use customer data to train their models under their commercial terms.

Image Storage (Cloudflare R2): Uploaded images are stored in Cloudflare R2 cloud storage. Images are associated with your account and are accessible only to you and our backend systems.

Fingerprint Generation (On Our Servers): For Slab Guard registrations, we generate a perceptual hash on our own servers. This is a one-way mathematical transformation — the hash cannot be reversed to recreate your image.

What we do NOT do with your images:

  • We do not share your images with other users
  • We do not sell your images or image data
  • We do not use facial recognition technology
  • We do not use your images for advertising

You can request deletion of your uploaded images at any time by contacting support@slabworthy.com.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Slab Worthy. Each has their own privacy policy governing how they handle data:

  • Amazon Web Services (AWS Rekognition): Automated image content moderation and safety screening. Images are processed in real-time and not retained by AWS after analysis.
  • Anthropic (Claude AI): AI-powered comic identification, condition analysis, and grading estimates.
  • Stripe: Payment processing for paid subscriptions. Stripe handles all credit card data directly — we never see or store your full card number.
  • Cloudflare: Hosting, CDN, and security services.
  • Cloudflare R2: Cloud image storage.
  • Render: Backend API hosting.
  • Resend: Transactional email delivery (password resets, account notifications).

We require all third-party service providers to handle your data in accordance with applicable privacy laws. We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

Data Retention

Account data: We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

Slab Guard registrations: If you delete your account or downgrade, your Slab Guard registrations are retained in our monitoring database for 90 days. After 90 days, they are permanently deleted. You can request immediate deletion by contacting us.

Images: Uploaded images are stored as long as your account is active. Images may be retained in anonymized form for AI model improvement. You can request deletion of specific images at any time.

Payment records: Transaction records are retained as required by tax and financial regulations (typically 7 years). Stripe retains payment data in accordance with their own retention policies.

Content moderation logs: Records of content safety screening (pass/fail results, not images) are retained for 12 months for security and compliance purposes.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@slabworthy.com.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged into your account
  • Remember your preferences
  • Understand how you use our service

You can control cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work properly if cookies are disabled.

Security

We take security seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect your data, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
  • Regular security reviews

However, no system is 100% secure. If you discover a security issue, please contact us immediately.

Children's Privacy

Slab Worthy requires users to be at least 16 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 16. If you believe we have collected information from someone under 16, please contact us immediately at support@slabworthy.com and we will delete the account and associated data promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our website or sending you an email. Your continued use of Slab Worthy after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

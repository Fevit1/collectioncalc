How This Works (No Copyright Issues)
These are CSS-only slab case silhouettes — no actual comic cover images. Each one is:

• A drawn CGC-style case shape with blue header bar and grade pill
• Abstract color gradients inside (red, blue, green, purple, orange) — evokes covers without copying any
• Generic issue numbers (#300, #1, #181, #94, #50) — numbers aren't copyrightable
• 12% opacity so they fade into the background
• Gentle floating animation so they feel alive
• 100% original IP — no Marvel, DC, or CGC trademarks

Note: The "CGC" text in the mockup would need to be changed to something generic like "SLAB" or removed entirely to avoid trademark issues. Shown here just to illustrate the visual concept.

If you want to go further, you could replace the abstract gradients with photos of your own slabs from your personal collection — that's perfectly legal and adds authenticity.

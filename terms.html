What Slab Worthy Does

Slab Worthy is an AI-powered tool that helps comic book collectors:

  • Estimate the condition/grade of raw (ungraded) comics using photo analysis
  • Calculate fair market value based on recent sales data
  • Determine whether professional grading (CGC®, CBCS®) makes financial sense
  • Generate auction listings for platforms like eBay and Whatnot

Important: Our "Slab Worthy?" feature is patent pending.

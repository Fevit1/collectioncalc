What is this?

Slab Guard™ uses advanced fingerprinting technology to track and recover stolen comics. Each comic registered with Slab Worthy receives a unique serial number that can be verified by potential buyers before purchase.

For buyers: Always verify the serial number before purchasing a high-value comic. If the status shows "Reported Stolen", do not purchase and report to authorities.

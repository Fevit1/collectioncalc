What is Slab Guard?

Slab Guard is our comic theft protection system. Register your comics with a unique digital fingerprint, and our Chrome extension monitors eBay listings in real time to alert you if a match appears. It's like a security system for your collection — and no one else offers it.

Questions?

Is there a free trial?
Yes! Pro, Guard, and Dealer plans all come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.
Can I change plans anytime?
Absolutely. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time. If you downgrade, you'll keep your current plan features until the end of your billing period.
What happens to my Slab Guard registrations if I downgrade?
Your registered comics stay in the system — we never delete them. If you exceed your new plan's limit, you won't be able to register new comics until you upgrade or remove some. Monitoring alerts will pause on the free plan.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes. If you're not happy within the first 30 days of a paid plan, we'll refund you in full — no questions asked.
I run a comic shop. Is the Dealer plan right for me?
If you buy/sell comics regularly and want to verify ownership before purchasing, the Dealer plan gives you the tools. The serial verification feature lets you check any Slab Guard serial instantly. Need a custom setup? Email us.
